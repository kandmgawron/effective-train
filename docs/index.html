FAQ

Where is my data stored? All workout data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.
Can I export my data? Yes. Go to History and tap Export to get a CSV of all your workouts.
How do progression recommendations work? The app tracks your reps and weight across sessions. When you consistently hit your rep range target, it suggests increasing weight. If you stall for 3 sessions, it suggests a deload.
I lost my data after updating/reinstalling Data is stored locally. Deleting the app removes all data. We recommend exporting regularly as a backup.

Privacy Policy

Gym Tracker Advanced does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All workout data remains on your device. The app does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking of any kind.

No account is required to use the app. No data is shared with third parties.
